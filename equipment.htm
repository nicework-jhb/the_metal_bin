Our Equipment

The Metal Bin is fully equipped to provide a comprehensive service to our clients. Our equipment ensures that there is minimum wastage and maximum value in our metal processing. We commit to producing high-quality, contamination-free metal.

Our equipment includes;

Two Kubota weighbridges: capable of handling up to 60 tons of metal and 18m trailers.
A 616 ton Lindemann Shear: has the capacity of processing 5 000 tons per month.
A Niton handheld analyser
A high density bailer
Granulators
Cutting torches
Conveyors
Magnetic sorters
Alligator shear
