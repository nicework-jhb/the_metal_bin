The Metal Bin is dedicated to servicing the waste needs of the metal industry.

The Metal Bin specialises in the recycling and processing of a variety of metals from ferrous to non-ferrous metals, mixed scrap and e-waste.

In operation since 1988, the Metal Bin has wide-ranging expertise to ensure we give our clients superior service at competitive prices.

Service delivery is important to us. We go the extra mile to ensure client satisfaction. We visit sites to discuss a solution that best suits the needs of our clients.
