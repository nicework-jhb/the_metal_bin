Qualifications

Qualifications The Metal Bin has been a proud member of the Metal Recyclers Association of South Africa (MRA) since 1994. The MRA, established in 1942, is the longest standing metal recycling association in South Africa.

MRA aims to encourage ethical trade, world-class service and greater communication of companies in the field of metal recycling. The organisation aims to promote the beneficial environmental impact of recycling as well as fighting against crime and theft in the industry.

Gavin Morgan (Head of Trading at The Metal Bin) has been part of the executive committee since 2008. The Metal Bin is committed to achieving MRA's aims.

The Metal Bin is also a member of the Corporate Employers association and abides by all the standards necessary to ensure that the workplace is a pleasant and safe one for all staff members.
