What We Do

At The Metal Bin we pride ourselves on following the best management practices. We offer a 24-hour collection or delivery turnaround on your metal. You can rely on us to provide you with an efficient and reliable service. We cater to industry, smaller metal processors and the individual.

Products and Services

Bin Placements

Our bin placements provide maximum return and fast turnaround for various commodities. Bin placements can be arranged for replacement at daily, weekly and monthly intervals. Our skip trucks collect any redundant scrap metal such as off-cuts, shavings and rejects.

Bulk Clearing

Our special bulk handling vehicles, with cranes and grapples, ensure the speedy collection of scrap from demolition sites.

Demolition

The Metal Bin dismantles, removes, and recovers, bulk scrap structures from demolition sites.

Destruction certificate

Upon request, we can provide photographic evidence of destruction for sensitive or redundant materials.

Non- Ferrous Metals

The Metal Bin has the capacity to process non-ferrous materials that include: aluminium, brass, copper, lead, zinc and stainless steel.

Ferrous Metals

The Metal Bin has the capacity to process ferrous materials that include: all grades of steel, cast iron, manganese cast and blue steel.

Unclean and Mixed Scrap

The Metal Bin specialises in the processing of unclean and mixed scrap metal. Slag, splashings, motors, cable scrap and a combination of ferrous and non-ferrous materials are included in this category.

E-waste

Our company has responded to the growing amount of electronic waste in need of disposal. The Metal Bin has the skills and knowledge to process and handle e-waste and cable scrap in an environmentally conscious manner.
