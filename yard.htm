The Metal Bin Yard

The Metal Bin is located in Jeppestown in the heart of the Johannesburg CBD. It is easily accessible to nearby major highways and the Kaserne Depot.

Gantry and cranes allow for the speedy turnaround of vehicles.

The entire yard is undercover ensuring that extreme weather conditions do not affect our trading.

Our loading dock is carefully designed to efficiently pack containers & vehicles for export and local markets.
